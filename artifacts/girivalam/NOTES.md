# Girivalam App — Notes

## Motto / Awakening Sentences
_The main motto behind the app — what it gives to people, what it makes people feel/become._

1. **Don't just deliver information — build a connection** with the person.

2. A person needs **clarity, peace of mind, and to know how to live**.

3. A person should know **how to live, how to face problems, how to stay strong alone**.

4. **Being spiritual is not easy.** It brings the person inside out — makes you alive, awakens you.

5. There are **some spots on earth where you feel zero thoughts** when you stand on them.

6. The main motto: **launch this Arunachala app, move more people toward Arunachala.**

7. Till now everyone has *visited* Arunachala — the real thing is to **make them understand what Arunachala actually is.**

8. **Build big features in phases.** If a feature is large, introduce it from phase 1 / prototype 1 in a basic form, then grow it in later stages.

---

## What this means for the app (founder's intent)
- The app is a **companion**, not a guidebook.
- It should help the person **arrive inside themselves**, not just arrive at the hill.
- Tone everywhere: **calm, alive, awakening — never tourist-app.**
- Build philosophy: **ship the seed of every big feature early, deepen it later.**
